About

Twenty years in Omaha. Eight in real estate.

I grew up in Bellevue, went to UNO, and have lived within thirty minutes of Memorial Park my whole adult life. I know which neighborhoods get the morning sun, which schools have waitlists, and which side of Dodge to avoid during rush hour.

What I love about this work is the moment someone walks into the right house and just knows. That's the part I'm here for. The negotiation, paperwork, inspections, lender hand-offs — those are my job. Your job is to find the place you want to live for the next ten years.

How I work

Patient, prepared, honest about what I see.

01

I take the time it takes.

I won't rush you into a house that doesn't fit. The right home is worth waiting for. I've had clients take two weeks and clients take eight months — both are fine.

02

I'll tell you what I see.

If a house has a foundation issue, a tough resale, or a school district that doesn't match what you said you wanted — I'll say so. Even if it costs me the deal.

03

I keep working after closing.

Need a plumber, a painter, or a recommendation for a contractor? I have a list. I want my clients to be neighbors for years, not transactions for a month.
